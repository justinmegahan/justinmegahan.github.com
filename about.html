About Fathoms

The information used on Fathoms is made available by the Florida Department of Education. It comes from the Florida Comprehensive Assessment Test, or FCAT.

The FCAT is a yearly statewide standardized test administered in Floridaâ€™s public schools.

Fathoms attempts to present information from public schools in the Tampa Bay Area in a manner that is easy to read and simple to navigate.

For more information on the FCAT visit these sites:

  • Florida Department of Education
  • Florida Comprehensive Assessment Test
  • Path to Success
The goal of Fathoms is to make school results from the Florida Comprehensive Assessment Test easily accessible for parents and students.
